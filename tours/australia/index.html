SRI LANKA

SAFARIS, SURFING AND INCREDIBLE CULTURE

A long hidden gem stashed among some of the world's most travelled and vibrant locales, Sri Lanka is a nation that's quickly making it's way onto everyone's bucket list and it's not hard to see why! From stunning white sandy beaches you'd expect to see on a remote tropical island to exotic wildlife safaris that rival the African plains, travelling Sri Lanka provides an incredible mix of of life changing experiences. Combine these with friendly locals, rolling green tea plantations and ancient temples, and you'll find a unique and unforgettable destination that will leave you wondering why you didn't visit sooner!

Discovering fascinating local culture, visiting ancient cave temples, strolling through endless tea plantations, travelling on the world's most scenic train journey, spotting wild elephants, crocodiles and amazing birdlife on safari, relaxing on gorgeous tropical beaches, learning to surf, teaching Buddhist monks English, and partying on the beach are just some of the unforgettable experiences you'll have while backpacking in Sri Lanka.

Travelling Sri Lanka is an epic adventure, and joining one of our small group tours like Sri Lanka Experience is the best way to experience it.

Whether you’re on a short holiday, starting a gap year or anything in between we’ll show you the best of this extraordinary country with some great new friends.
