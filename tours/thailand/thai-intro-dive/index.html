HIGHLIGHTS


Experience crazy Bangkok nightlife, learn to cook delicious Thai food,Â go tubing, kayaking, sleep overnight on floating bungalows as well asÂ Scuba Diving on the stunning island of Koh Tao while getting your PADI Open Water Dive Certification.

WHERE YOU GO
DAY 1: ARRIVE AND MEET THE GROUP

We'll pick you up from the airport and take you to the guesthouse in Bangkok. You'll then have plenty of time to recover from your long flight, chill out and meet everyone in your group.

DAY 2: RIVER CRUISE & TEMPLES

Discover Bangkok from a different perspective as we take a relaxing cruise through the heart of the city. We'll then visit Bangkok's most sacred and ancient temples and travel by Tuk Tuk through the crazy streets! At night we'll head out and experience some of the best nightlife in the world.

DAY 3: THAI COOKING CLASS & OVERNIGHT TRAIN

Learn the secrets to preparing delicious Thai cuisine first hand with a traditional Thai cooking class. Then you can sit back, relax and enjoy the sights of Thailand when we board the overnight train and travel down south.

DAY 4: KHAO SOK NATIONAL PARK

We'll wind through amazing limestone cliffs in a long-tail boat to a pristine freshwater lake and will stay overnight in floating bungalows. You'll also go kayaking, tubing and monkey spotting!

DAY 5: ARRIVE IN KOH PHANGAN & BEACH PARTY

We'll travel by boat to the beautiful tropical island of Koh Phangan -home of the world famous full?moon parties. After checking in to our own amazing beachside bungalows you can spend the afternoon exploring the island or just relax by the pool and work on your tan. We'll then head out and party on the world famous full moon party beach!

DAY 6: TRADITIONAL THAI MASSAGE & BEACH TIME

A day to unwind, relax and get some well-deserved beach time. Before being pampered with a traditional Thai massage.

DAY 7: ISLAND BOAT TRIP, SNORKELLING & WATERFALLS

You'll discover the beauty of the island as we travel by long-tail boat to some of the most amazing beaches in the world. You'll go snorkelling in crystal clear water and take a short trek through the jungle to swim under waterfalls.

DAY 8: MUAY THAI OR YOGA EXPERIENCE

Today you have the choice of learning the art of Muay Thai boxing or bringing some peace and harmony into your action packed trip with a private Yoga class. We'll then head out for our last night on Koh Phangan.

DAY 9: ARRIVE IN KOH TAO

Arrive in Koh Tao at anytime. Meet at the ferry pier for your pickup and transfer to your guesthouse. After your orientation, you'll get stuck into your SCUBA diving theory. Lessons are conducted in a fun, friendly way.

DAY 10: OPEN WATER DIVE COURSE (SHALLOW WATER TRAINING)

Heading out to one of the islands many beautiful bays, the course continues with a shallow, confined water skills session. You'll learn how to use your equipment, get used to breathing underwater and will learn how to control your buoyancy.

DAY 11: OPEN WATER DIVE COURSE (12 METRES)

We'll visit two amazing dive spots where you'll dive to a maximum depth of 12 metres. As part of these dives you'll get to explore the dive sites and will see lots of bright corals, tropical fish and maybe even a turtle or two!

DAY 12: OPEN WATER DIVE COURSE (18 METRES)

We'll head out to the famous Chumpon Pinnacles to see bigger schools of fish, reef sharks and maybe even a whale shark! Diving to 18 metres, you'll have the chance to star in your very own underwater movie as our videographers capture your experience.

DAY 13: CHECK OUT & 2 FUN DIVES

Check out that morning then put your new skills to the test as you enjoy 2 more dives! Then continue your travels or extend your stay on Koh Tao.
